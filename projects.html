The Land

Quantified Self tumblr Fall 2015 - Spring 2016
Three CoLab members, Jackie Cameron, Micha Gorelick, and Michael Skirpan, co-produced an immersive theater piece about social issues involving data and technology. Produced with the help of University of Colorado, Fast Forward Labs, and the John S. and James L. Knight Foundation, the production used audience member data to personalize 10 interactive art installations inside the set as a sci-fi drama happened around them.

CoLab played an important role in the initial brainstorming, script writing process, and final design and set build.
